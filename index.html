Bee pollen products.

Bee pollen is a natural food rich in vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients. The use of bee pollen has spread among men and women in order to treat various health problems and diseases that afflict them.

Bee pollen is a natural mixture that contains flower pollen and nectar, bee secretions and enzymes, as well as honey and wax. Pollen is usually found on the bodies of bees, as it is collected from plants and transported to the beehive in order to be used as food for bees. There is a difference between bee pollen and other products extracted from bees, natural honey, royal jelly, honeycombs made of beeswax, and bee venom.
